# AI Jobs Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an AI Jobs Analytics Dashboard created in Excel to analyze job market trends, salary patterns, remote work opportunities, and demand across different AI job categories and industries. The dashboard helps understand the current AI hiring landscape using interactive visuals and summary metrics.

## Problem Statement
The objective of this project was to analyze AI job postings and identify:
- The most in-demand job categories
- The share of remote and non-remote jobs
- The average salary offered across job roles
- The percentage of AI/LLM roles in the job market
- Industry-wise demand trends
- Year-over-year demand growth
- Salary differences based on seniority, experience, and job type

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains detailed AI job market records with the following fields:

- job_id
- job_title
- job_category
- experience_level
- years_of_experience
- education_required
- annual_salary_usd
- salary_min_usd
- salary_max_usd
- city
- country
- remote_work
- company_size
- industry
- required_skills
- ai_salary_premium_pct
- demand_score
- demand_growth_yoy_pct
- benefits_score_10
- posting_year
- posting_month
- is_senior
- is_remote_friendly
- is_llm_role
- salary_tier

These fields were used to analyze salary trends, demand patterns, remote work opportunities, role seniority, and industry-wise hiring behavior in the AI job market.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Dashboard Design

## Process
- Cleaned and organized the raw dataset in Excel
- Created pivot tables to summarize job categories, salaries, and industry demand
- Built pivot charts for visual analysis
- Added slicers for interactive filtering by experience level, posting year, and country
- Designed a dashboard to present insights in a clear and structured way

## Dashboard Highlights
- Total Jobs: 1,500
- Average Annual Salary: $194,892
- Remote Jobs Percentage: 75.40%
- AI/LLM Jobs Percentage: 21.80%
- Average Demand Score: 8.80

## Key Insights
- AI Engineering is the most in-demand category with 736 job postings.
- Remote jobs account for 75.4% of the total dataset, showing strong remote hiring trends.
- AI/LLM roles make up 21.8% of total jobs, while non-AI roles account for 78.2%.
- The average annual salary is $194,892, indicating strong earning potential in AI-related roles.
- AI Engineering has the highest average salary among job categories.
- Industries such as Healthcare, Automotive, Government, and Finance show strong hiring demand.
- Job demand in 2026 is higher than 2025 across most categories, indicating positive market growth.

## Conclusion
This Excel dashboard provides a clear overview of AI job market trends and helps identify high-demand roles, salary patterns, remote work opportunities, and industry hiring behavior. It demonstrates practical skills in Excel, data analysis, dashboard creation, and insight generation.
